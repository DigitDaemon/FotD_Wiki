Evocation:

Decide what you want to do: Attack, Shield, Gain Advantage, Counterspell

Attack:

  1. Decide how much power your spell needs: 2 shifts of power for each point of damage you want to do (you can split this damage between multiple targets); 2 additional shifts of power allows you to hit every creature in a zone with your spell. (SP)
  2. Subtract your Spellcasting and stunt bonuses from the shift power total, you take Mental stress equal to that number + 1 (SP - Spellcasting - Stunt Bonuses + 1)(Min 1)
  3. Roll your Will and subtract the roll from the power of the spell (SP - Will Roll = Overflow) [Enemies use the Will roll as the target to avoid the attack]
  4. If there is positive Overflow, you can choose to either take the Overflow as physical stress or risk collateral damage around you based on the strength of the Overflow

Shield:

A Shield is an Evocation-based full defense that costs one mental stress. Make a Spellcasting roll at +2, as usual for a full defense. If an attack manages to inflict enough shifts to overcome your armor, the shield breaks and you cannot block attacks with it anymore. This spell lasts until it is dropped or broken at no additional stress cost, but you cannot take any action except defense while itâ€™s active.

Gain Advantage:

  1. Decide what you want to do, the GM will give you a cost for that effect. (SP)
  2. Subtract your Spellcasting and Stunt bonuses from the shift power total, you take Mental stress equal to that number + 1 (SP - Spellcasting - Stunt Bonuses + 1)(Min 1)
  3. There may be additional things depending on what effect you cause.

Counterspell:

  1. Decide how much power your counterspell needs (Assessment roll). (SP)
  2. Subtract your Spellcasting and Stunt Bonuses from the shift power total, you take Mental stress equal to that number + 1 (SP - Spellcasting - Stunt Bonuses + 1)(Min 1)
  3. Roll your Will and subtract the roll from the power of the spell (SP - Will Roll = Overflow)
  4. If there is positive Overflow, you can choose to either take the Overflow as physical stress or risk collateral damage around you based on the strength of the Overflow

Factions


Paranet:

The Paranet is a global community of weaker magical talents that previously were preyed upon by larger magical threats. Through sharing of talent and information, members of the Paranet have been able to effectively take down magical threats and protect vulnerable people and communities though they rely to an extent on other local factions for protection like Harry Dresden or Johnny Marcone's gang in Chicago.


Snowblind Limited:

Snowblind is a corporate front for Winter's dealings in the greater part of Canada and the Northern US. Winter Fae are unable to deal with mortals directly, but the Winter Lady, Molly Carpenter has some leniency and the Winter Knight, Harry Dresden has no such restriction. Between the two of them pressuring the accorded nations into paying restitution to the citizens of Chicago, the Winter Court has taken some measures to stop something similar happening in the future. Official Snowblind is a cold weather research and development contractor, giving them the excuse for doing strange things in strange places all over the cold climate regions of North America.


Winter Court of Fairy:

The Winter Court is large and powerful. Like The Summer court it is controlled by the three Winter Queens: Mother Winter, Mab the Winter Queen, and Molly the Winter Lady. The winter fey are known for being perfect in their love of art and cruel and malicious in their treatment of mortals, representing death and the end of things. However the winter court is vital to the balance of the mortal world and an integral part of defending reality from what lies beyond the outer gates.


Summer Court of Fairy:

The Summer Court is not as large as the Winter Court but more direct in its involvement with mortal kind. Like the Winter Court it is controlled by the three Summer Queens:Mother Summer, Titania the Summer Queen and Lady Sarissa. The summer fey are warm and welcoming, encouraging growth and life. However, they can be just as dangerous as the winter fey, after all disease causing bacteria and poisonous flowers are alive as well. The Summer Court exists to balance the power of the Winter Court and to shield the mortal worlds from the worst of Winterâ€™s cruelty.


White Court Vampires:

White vampires are loosely organized into the White Court. The White Court is currently ruled over by Lord Raith the head of House Raith with the two other large families being House Malvora and House Skavis. House Raith vampires learn to feed off lust, Malvora off of Fear and Skavis off of Despair. The large houses of the White court are in constant rivalry for power and the seat of ruler but tend to operate mostly with business dealings and corporate intrigue.
