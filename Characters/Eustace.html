Eustice Kingston is the local crime boss in Toronto. He unified all the gangs and criminal elements in the city. Despite the possible profitability, he cut out dangerous, low quality drugs from his supply and instructs his dealers to cut off junkies who are letting their addiction get the best of them. He also has restricted the sales of illegal weapons quite a bit. It seems that he believes that if there is goign to be illegal activities, they might as well be run by someone with the moral code to look out for th ecity.

Eustace's daughter was killed in an incident at a warehouse and her body was discovered by the party, Eustace has a understandable intresting in finding out exactly what happened, and what part Snowblind played int it.

Eustace has offered the player pay for information on a rival crime lord that seems to be trying to move in on Toronto. He sees Johny Marcone as not only a rival but a danger to the city and wants to get rid of him quickly and quietly.
