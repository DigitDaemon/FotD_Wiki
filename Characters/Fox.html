Fox the Grey is a less Native American deity/spirit with domain over forested areas. He is specifically charged with playing tricks and punishing people who break their promises and abandon their responcibilities. Fox is the patron of player character Mason Bishop. Fox has said on several occasions that he picked Mason to be his emissary because Mason embodies Fox's purpose, a man who runs from his problems rather than solving them.

Fox currently is occupied with discovering why "poisonous human made glass" is apearring in the city of Toronto and especially the surrounding wilderness.
