Valentine Ramero is the 'Boss" of the Howl at the Moon Coffee Shop, a quiet little coffee shop in the eastern resdential district of Toronto. The coffee shop seems to be a front for some sort of supernatural vigilante group. Valentine is egoistic and aloof, looking down on the other people of the supernatural community as monsters and trouble makers, unworthy of consideration.
